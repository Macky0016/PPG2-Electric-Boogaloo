Speed:
Shift G to Increase Speed
Shift H to Decrease Speed

Gun Controlling:
Press Freeze on the Gun, Press the Control Gun Button.
Left Click to Shoot.
To Rotate the gun simple pause time and rotate.

Disable Guts:
Run the game atleast once with the mod.
Close the game.
Navigate to your People Playground Folder, you will see a file called settings, open it.
Change doGuts : true to doGuts : false
Do the opposite to re enable guts.
